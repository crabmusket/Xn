Xn

Dungeon generator

About

Xn is a program that procedurally generates "dungeons" by arranging rooms of predefined shape in a cubic grid.

Its name is inspired by the X16, X18 and X19 laboratories in S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl.

Click and drag the left mouse button to rotate your view of the generated level. Click and drag the right button to zoom, and use the scroll wheel to move the camera focus vertically.

Red cubes represent parts of a room, green squares represent doors, and blue planes are corridors.

Made with Python, jQuery and WebGLU.
